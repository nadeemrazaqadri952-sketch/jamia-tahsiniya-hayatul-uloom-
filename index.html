About Jamia

Jamia Tehsiniya Hayat Ul Uloom Lil Banat Bareilly Sharif mein waqia ek azeem idara hai jo 2015 se deeni wa duniyavi taleem ki khidmat anjaam de raha hai. Yahan bachchiyon ko Quran, Hadees, Fiqh ke saath Hindi, English, Math aur Computer ki taleem bhi di jati hai.

Principal's Message

Bismillahir Rahmanir Rahim. Hamara maqsad bachchiyon ko ilm, akhlaq aur deeni shaoor se aarasta karna hai. Hum Quran-o-Sunnat ki roshni mein ek behtareen nasl tayyar karne ke liye koshish kar rahe hain.
